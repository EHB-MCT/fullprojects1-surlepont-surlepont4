Who are we

Surlepont is a group of inhabitants of Anderlecht who like the Pierre Marchant bridge as a viewing platform rest-point, green urban and social place.

Soon there will be a decision about the future of the bridge. Surlepont wants to give all inhabitants and peasants a voice who value this place and sustainable mobility
