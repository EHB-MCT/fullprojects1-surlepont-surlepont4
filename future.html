Future

Pierre Marchant bridge

Renders

At the end of 2022, the Brussels Region proposed a project for the Pierre Marchant bridge. At the beginning of 2023, a public inquiry was underway and residents were able to express their views on the new plans. These show a bridge that is no longer used by cars, with an asphalt cycle path in the center and a wooden deck for pedestrians on both sides. There are also mentions of benches, picnic tables and temporary elements, so the proposed plans seem in line with our visions! We have created a petition to show our support. This petition has been signed by x people. Surlepont is a supporter of this project and responded in writing and orally to the consultation committee of 16 February 2023.

Public space around the bridge

Beliris will redevelop all public spaces in the new Biestebroek district. You see two visualizations from a preliminary study by Beliris of December 2021. The Grondelsstraat will become a green public space, only accessible to pedestrians and cyclists, from the Pierre Marchant bridge to the railway bridge. The Grondelsplein just on the other side of the railway bridge will also be completely car-free.
